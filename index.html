Our Mission is Simple...

To make billions of people feel more alive.

Too often, technology builds for an abstract notion of humanity. We’re building for the actual humans that make up humanity--the ones figuring it out, hoping, becoming, living. Robyn wasn’t conceived in a hackathon or ideation session. Robyn was conceived when our founder, a Harvard-trained MD, saw the loneliness and disconnection in her patients. Against a growing tide of loneliness and hype about AI replacing people--a notion we categorically reject--Robyn is building the opposite: a place to be deeply and unapologetically human.

See the patterns running you in real time. Own your story.

Your conversations tell a story. Robyn turns insights from your chats, voice notes, and moods into a real-time mirror so you can visualize your journey. We believe patterns become power when you start seeing them clearly, and Robyn is here to show you your evolving patterns in real time.
